Directions

There is a gate on the west side, but there are actually two gates — one to the north and one to the south (the Harajuku Station side).
Please use the south gate. Do not ring the doorbell at the north gate under any circumstances.

You can enter from the location indicated by the arrow, but please do not go inside until 10 minutes before the event starts.
Once you enter, be sure to take off your shoes and go up the stairs.

For table tenns: Ground floor
For other sports: Go up the stairs.
